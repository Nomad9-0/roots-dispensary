Your First Visit!

1. Welcome to the roots!

2. Check In

When you first walk into our shop, you will be greeted by our friendly receptionist. Provide a valid, government-issued picture identification and we will get you set up in our system. All your personal information is secured and kept in-house only for legal compliance. You also have the ability to leave your phone number and email address to stay up to date with our latest promotions, giveaways, and product drops!

3. Meet Your Budtender

Once you are all checked in, one of our trained and talented budtenders will assist you. Our staff has over 30 years of combined experience in the traditional and recreational cannabis market. We are highly educated in all aspects of cannabis products, from edibles to concentrates & tinctures to flower. Our budtenders take pride in engaging in professional development to stay on the cutting edge of the cannabis industry and are diverse cast of people ready to answer your questions. Our customer service is unparalleled, and we will help you find the right product at the right price.

4. Explore

Take a look around the shop! We are more than happy to give new and existing customers the tour of our shop and introduce them to the wide variety of products we carry. Our ample & modern showroom floor is clean and inviting. No more having to lurk around dimly lit backrooms! We are also taking advanced steps in light of COVID-19, such as increased sanitation, limited occupancy, and facemask requirements.

5. Checkout

Once you find the products you are looking for, you are all set! At checkout, you have the opportunity to pay with cash or debit. Don't worry, we also have an ATM inside. In a hurry or want to skip the line? You can place a pickup order online or over the phone and pay up front at our express checkout. If you enjoyed your visit, make sure to leave us a five-star review on Weedmaps or bring in a friend next time you stop by. Either option will you 20% off your next purchase.

6. Enjoy!
