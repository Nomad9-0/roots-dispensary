Flower

Flower

Pot. Weed. Ganja. Whatever you want to call it, a flower by any other name is just as sweet. With over 40+ cultivars on our shelves, you're certain to find the right one. Browse our selection of indoor, outdoor, and mixed lighting grown flower. We have fresh flower from some of the biggest and most reputable growers in the state of California.

Cartridges

Cartridges

Cannabis cartridges provide you with a discrete, convenient way to medicate on the go. Take the potency of concentrates with you without the hassle of bulky equipment. Our stock of cannabis oil cartridges is diverse and include several choices of both premium distillate and live resin.

Edibles

Edibles

Many of our patrons describe being in one shop like being in a candy store and our selection of edibles will show you why. From gummies to chocolates and baked goods as well, there is something to satisfy your cravings. Don't worry about unintentionally overdoing it - everything is accurately dosed& formulated so you know exactly what you're getting.

Extract

Extract

Cannabis extracts have come a long way from your grandpa’s hash. For the connoisseur who is looking for high potencies in small packages, we have you covered. Whether you’re a solventless hash head, a live resin ripper, or a BHO shatter dabber, our selection of concentrates and extracts is the envy of any 710 enthusiast. We keep our dabs in the fridge to ensure that you experience them at their optimum quality and taste profile.

Preroll

Preroll

Perfect for people who are on the go. Whether you’re a novice or a seasoned expert, prerolls provide you with a consistent and convenient way to access premium flower. If you’re looking for something with an extra punch, our selection of wax- and kief-infused prerolls will get you to where you need to be. We also carry packs of prerolls if you need them for your next road trip, movie night, or any other occasion.

Topical

Topical

Our selection of cannabis-infused topicals are made with only the finest ingredients and are optimized to provide targeted relief for chronic and acute pain. Using traditional ingredients with state-of-the-art science, our topical selection may help with the management of pain and skin conditions without providing any of the psychoactive effects associated with cannabis.
