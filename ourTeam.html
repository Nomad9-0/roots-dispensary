Situated in the Lompoc Valley, TRD holds a record of excellent and consistent service to Ventura and Santa Barbara Counties. Our primary aim is to offer safe and convenient access to qualified patients who seek high-quality, scientifically tested medical cannabis. Having passed through pure analytics and extensive screening processes, our lab-tested products are proven free from all foreign materials and hence fit for consumption.

Our competent team offers both excellent individual and collective experience and expertise, to satisfy our esteemed customers to the letter. This we aim to achieve by providing excellent one-on-one patient consulting. In a bid to maintain our position as a great community asset; we ensure the delivery of dispensary and recreational facilities and services for the safe obtainment of medically-prescribed cannabis and unparalleled enjoyment of quality cannabis products.

In pursuance of our resolution of continued advocacy for patient’s rights and responsible public policy, we promote health and empowerment through access and education. Apart from establishing sustainable business with a locally-directed focus that enhances communities financially and aesthetically, we also build strong, positive relationships with all stakeholders, including patients, vendors, residents, community leaders and others.

By matching cutting-edge cannabis science with rich and practical knowledge of our versatile Executive and Advisory Board, we conveniently develop a unique collaboration that improves access to cannabis as well as emphasizing pleasant and safe cannabis consumption and positive public health outcomes.
